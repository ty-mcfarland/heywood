About Dr. Heywood is a licensed psychologist who maintains a private practice in West Palm Beach,Florida specializing in the treatment of anxiety, mood, and interpersonal disorders with adults and children. He has practiced in Palm Beach County for over 20 years, working in schools, psychiatric facilities, and the private sector. Dr. Heywood moved to Florida in 1996 to serve his Doctoral Residency training at a community psychiatric hospital in WPB following the completion of his clinical internship at the Veterans Affairs Medical Center in Virginia. Here he continued for the next 6 years providing neuropsychological evaluations, supervision, and psychotherapy intervention services.
